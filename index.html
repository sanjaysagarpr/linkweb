About Me

Who am I?

I’m Sanjay P R, a passionate and detail-oriented interior designer dedicated to creating functional, aesthetic, and personalized spaces. With a strong understanding of materials, textures, and spatial planning, I blend creativity with practicality to deliver designs that reflect my clients' lifestyles and aspirations. Whether it's residential, commercial, or bespoke interiors, my focus is always on bringing ideas to life with innovation and integrity.

With a strong understanding of spatial planning, materials, and lighting, I bring a balanced approach to every project—from concept development to final execution. I’m proficient in leading design software including AutoCAD, SketchUp, 3ds Max, V-Ray, Enscape, Revit and Photoshop allowing me to visualize and present ideas clearly and effectively.

Driven by innovation, attention to detail, and a commitment to quality, I strive to deliver interiors that enhance everyday living and elevate the human experience.
